## Collect
Lord God, the unfailing helper and guide of those whom you bring up in your steadfast fear and love, keep us, we pray, under the protection of your good providence, and give us a continual reverence and love for your holy name; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

| **From the Passage** |**Heavenly Father, Creator of the hidden depths,**
<br><br>We look at the world around us and marvel at human ingenuity. We have mined the earth for silver and refined gold from the dust. We have put an end to darkness, exploring the farthest recesses to uncover hidden treasures that no bird of prey has seen and no proud beast has trodden. We are so skilled at searching for the things of this earth.
<br><br>Yet, Lord, we confess that we often search for wisdom in all the wrong places. We ask, along with Job, "Where can wisdom be found? Where does understanding dwell?" It is not found in the land of the living, nor can it be bought with the finest gold, choice silver, or precious jewels. All the wealth and achievement of this world cannot equal its worth.
<br><br>Remind us, O God, that You alone understand its way; You alone know where it dwells. You looked to the ends of the earth and saw everything under the heavens. You gave the wind its weight and meted out the waters by measure.
<br><br>Turn our hearts away from the proud strivings of our own minds, and bring us back to the ultimate truth You revealed to humankind:"The fear of the Lord—that is wisdom, and to shun evil is understanding."
<br><br>Grant us a healthy awe and reverence for who You are. Let our search for understanding begin with a humble heart that honors Your majesty. Give us the discernment to turn away from evil, to seek righteousness, and to trust Your hidden ways when our own understanding fails.
<br><br>May Your true wisdom guide our steps today and always.
<br><br>Amen.|
