## Collect
God our Father, you have prepared for those who love you such good things as pass man’s understanding: pour into our hearts such love toward you, that we, loving you above all things, may obtain your promises which exceed all that we can desire; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

## Week E

| **Section** | **Content** |
|--------------|-------------|
| **From the Passage** |Heavenly Father, Creator of the Stars and Oceans, <br><br> We stand in awe of Your incredible greatness today! You are the Master of life, the One who spoke the universe into existence and handcrafted every single one of us.<br><br> You designed our hearts to respond to brilliant things. We thank You for the good gifts of human talent, beautiful creation, and the amazing skills we get to cheer for in this world.<br><br> But Father, we confess that we often get so caught up in cheering for these things that we forget You—the ultimate Source of all greatness. Forgive us for the times we treat people like gods, or focus too much on our own greatness instead of pointing to Yours. Thank You for being a God of second chances who loves and rescues us when we are messy and helpless.<br><br> We praise You for the ultimate gift of Your Son, Jesus. Thank You for His awesome wonders, for dying on the cross for our mistakes, and for defeating death by rising again three days later.<br><br> Because of this beautiful rescue, help us to be Your ultimate superfans. Teach us to enjoy Your creation while always pointing straight past it to You, shouting: *"Look at how great our God is!"* <br><br> Help us to trust Your Ten Commandments, knowing You are the ultimate Expert of life who knows the absolute best way for us to live. This week, help us to glorify You by **enjoying** You, **loving** You, **trusting** You, and **obeying** Your commands with glad hearts.<br><br> In the mighty name of Jesus, the Greatest of All Time, we pray, Amen.|
| **Mission & Vision** | **For Growing Deep** <br><br>Dear God, Jesus reminded us that the greatest thing we can do in this life is to love You with all of our heart, soul, mind, and strength. Thank You for giving us these first four commandments to show us exactly how to do that. We pray that our lives would show that You hold the first-place trophy of our hearts, even when it’s hard or uncomfortable to choose You. Help us to put away our backward magnifying glasses. Forgive us for the times we try to shrink You down or try to be the boss of You. We want to love You for the big, wild, spectacular God You truly are.<br><br> As we leave this place and head into our week, help us to wear Your jersey with pride and respect. Let our words and our actions at school, at work, and at home show the world how good our Team Captain is. <br><br>And finally, Lord, help us to put down our keys and pause our busy tasks so that we can give You our full concentration. Thank You that You run the universe perfectly well without our help, and that we can safely rest in Your care. Search our hearts today, and show each of us which of these four areas You want us to work on this week. We love You, Lord, and we want to follow Your way. <br><br>In Jesus' name we pray, Amen.|
| **Global & Nation** | **Nation -  A Prayer for Venezuela** <br><br> Gracious God, our refuge and strength, we pray for the people of Venezuela following the recent earthquakes. Comfort those who mourn, heal the injured, protect those still awaiting rescue, and strengthen all who are working to bring relief and care. Give wisdom to those leading the response, generosity to those able to help, and hope to all who are facing loss and uncertainty. We pray especially for those who know and love you in Venezuela: strengthen their trust in you, fill them with compassion, and use them to point others to the hope that is found in the Lord Jesus Christ. <br><br> In his name we pray. Amen.|

| **Church Ministries** | **Evangelism teams** <br><br> Gracious Lord, we thank You for giving us opportunities to proclaim Your name to those who are lost and far from You. Please continue to open our eyes to see the people You have placed around us, and grant us love, courage, and wisdom to make the most of every opportunity to bear witness to Christ in our daily lives. Lord, prepare the hearts of those we come into contact with. Remove every barrier that keeps them from knowing You, and help them to be open to hearing the message of the Gospel. May You use our words, our actions, and our lives as a testimony to Your grace and truth, leading many to know Jesus Christ. <br><br> Strengthen and unite our evangelism team as we serve together in outreach, invitations, and follow-up ministries. Help us not to rely on our own strength, but on the power of the Holy Spirit, faithfully sowing the seeds of the Gospel and patiently trusting You for the harvest. <br><br> May You continue to use our church throughout this season, so that more people may come to know You, experience Your love, and receive the new life and hope that are found in Christ. Amen.|
| **Groups in Church** | **Cantonese congregation** <br><br> Heavenly Father, we thank You for faithfully leading our Cantonese congregation. Throughout the past months, we have experienced Your faithfulness and grace through Your Word, our fellowship, and our service together. Continue to build us up, so that we may not only know about You, but truly know You as the one true God and faithfully bear witness to You in our daily lives. <br><br> Help us to delight in Your Word and gladly obey Your will. May we not only understand Your teaching, but also live lives that honour You, love You, and love others in our families, workplaces, church, and community, bringing glory to Your name. Strengthen and unite all our ministry teams. Teach us not to rely on our own strength, but to serve together in the power of the Holy Spirit, encouraging and building one another up in love. May everything we do bear witness to the love and truth of Christ. <br><br> May our church truly be a community that knows You and makes You known, so that through our lives and our witness, many may come to know Christ and receive the hope, new life, and salvation that are found in You alone. Amen.|
