## Collect
Almighty God, grant that we, who justly deserve to be punished for our sinful deeds, may in your mercy and kindness be pardoned and restored; through our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. Amen.

## Week C

| **Section** | **Content** |
|--------------|-------------|
| **From the Passage** | ​Heavenly Father, <br> ​We confess that we so often live our lives in the rush of the "subway concourse." We are distracted by the noise, hurried by our schedules, and blinded by our own expectations of who we think You should be. We admit that we have often brushed past Your glory, failing to hear the music of the Master playing right in our midst. ​Lord Jesus, ​We thank You for the Mount of Transfiguration. Thank You for pulling back the veil and showing us that You are not just a teacher, a prophet, or a good man—but the Radiant King of Glory. We thank You for the "Verification" You provided: <br> <br>- ​For the Transfigured Light that proves You are God from God.<br>- ​For the Witness of Moses and Elijah, showing us that all of history points to You. <br>- ​For the Father’s Voice, confirming Your beloved authority. <br>- ​And for the Forerunner, who reminds us that Your path to the throne went through the Cross. <br>- As we descend from the mountain of this message and return to the "commute" of our daily lives, give us ears to listen to Him. <br> <br> May His voice rise above the noise of our culture and our own anxieties. Teach us to enjoy the "music" of His grace and to live as people who have truly seen the King. <br> ​In the name of Jesus, our Transfigured Lord, <br>Amen.|
