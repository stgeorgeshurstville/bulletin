## Collect
Lord of all power and might, the author and giver of all good things, graft in our hearts the love of your Name, increase in us true religion, nourish us with all goodness, and so by your mercy keep us; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

| **From the Passage** |Dear Heavenly Father, <br><br> Thank you that you made us, and thank you that every single person is deeply precious and valuable to you. You created us with a beautiful purpose: to love you and to reflect your goodness to the world around us. We confess that we often fall short of that purpose. If we are honest, we often settle for doing the bare minimum. We hold onto our pride, our anger, and our own selfish wants instead of giving love, respect, and kindness to our neighbors. Thank you for the wonderful news of Jesus. Thank you that He showed us what perfect love looks like, and that through His sacrifice on the cross, He offers us forgiveness, heals our brokenness, and gives us a fresh start. <br><br> Fill us with your Holy Spirit this week. Change our hearts so that we stop holding on to selfishness and start living generously. Help us to honor our families, to forgive those who hurt us, to keep our promises, to speak the truth in love, and to be content with what we have. Don't let us settle for just staying out of trouble or doing the least amount required. Help us to love actively, seeking ways to lift others up and show them your grace. <br><br> In Jesus’ name we pray, **Amen.**|
