## Collect
Almighty God, we pray that the course of this world may be so peaceably ordered through your guidance that your church may joyfully serve you in all godly quietness; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

| **From the Passage** |**Heavenly Father, Creator of the Stars and Oceans,** <br><br> We stand in awe of Your incredible greatness today! You are the Master of life, the One who spoke the universe into existence and handcrafted every single one of us.<br> You designed our hearts to respond to brilliant things. We thank You for the good gifts of human talent, beautiful creation, and the amazing skills we get to cheer for in this world.<br> But Father, we confess that we often get so caught up in cheering for these things that we forget You—the ultimate Source of all greatness. Forgive us for the times we treat people like gods, or focus too much on our own greatness instead of pointing to Yours. Thank You for being a God of second chances who loves and rescues us when we are messy and helpless.<br> We praise You for the ultimate gift of Your Son, Jesus. Thank You for His awesome wonders, for dying on the cross for our mistakes, and for defeating death by rising again three days later.<br> Because of this beautiful rescue, help us to be Your ultimate superfans. Teach us to enjoy Your creation while always pointing straight past it to You, shouting: *"Look at how great our God is!"* <br> Help us to trust Your Ten Commandments, knowing You are the ultimate Expert of life who knows the absolute best way for us to live. This week, help us to glorify You by **enjoying** You, **loving** You, **trusting** You, and **obeying** Your commands with glad hearts.<br> In the mighty name of Jesus, the Greatest of All Time, we pray, <br>Amen.|
