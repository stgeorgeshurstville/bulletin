## Collect
We beseech you, almighty God, to look in mercy on your people: that your great goodness they may be governed and preserved evermore; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

| **From the Passage** | ​Most gracious God, Father and Fountain of mercy, who has blessed us with the knowledge of our true happiness and the way that leads to it. We confess that we have often wandered, contenting ourselves with the "vain shadows" of religion and using your holy Word to justify our own selfish ends rather than to know you.<br>
We thank you that you sent your Son to dwell among us, not only to be our sacrifice but to be the "perfect pattern" of what we are meant to be. We pray that he would be truly "formed within us," so that we are saved not just from judgment, but for righteousness. Save us from the joyless burden of legalism and the empty license of cheap grace. <br>
Possess our hearts with a "holy disdain" for the poor enjoyments of this world, and fill us with an "ardent love" for you. May this love enlarge our hearts toward all mankind, that we may love our neighbors—even our enemies—sincerely for your sake. <br>
Grant that the consideration of who you are, and what we are, would humble us and stir up in us an "unmixed joy and an unbounded love". Guide us with your counsel, and afterward receive us into glory, through the merits of our Savior, Jesus Christ.<br>Amen.|
