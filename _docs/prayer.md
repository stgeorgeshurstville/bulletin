## Collect
Graciously hear us, Lord God; and grant that we, to whom you have given the desire to pray, may by your mighty aid be defended and strengthened in all dangers and adversities; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

| **From the Passage** |Almighty God and heavenly Father, <br><br> You are mighty in power and wise in judgment. <br><br> Sometimes we feel that you are far away, especially in the midst of pain and suffering. With the psalmist we cry: “How long, O Lord? Will you forget me forever? How long will you hide your face from me? How long must I take counsel in my soul and have sorrow in my heart all the day?” <br><br> We cry this for ourselves and for the many who are sitting in the ashes of real grief—those facing sudden diagnoses, broken relationships, financial anxiety, evil and violence, and the heavy silence. <br><br> In the Gospel, you have shown so clearly that you have never been distant nor indifferent. In our Lord, Jesus, crucified for us, You stepped into our chaos, carried our sorrows, and met us up close. <br><br> We need wisdom to live well. <br><br> We trust in your steadfast love; our heart shall rejoice in your salvation. Even, and especially, through the storm. <br><br> n Jesus' name, <br><br> Amen.|

| **Church Ministries** | **CanCare and Nursing home/Pastoral Care Teams** <br><br> Heavenly Father, Heavenly Father, we praise You for Your kindness and compassion. You understand what it means to face trials and suffering, and You sympathize with our weaknesses. We thank You for using CanCare Centre to support those who are currently battling or have battled cancer, as well as their families and friends. Through the love of Christ, they are cared for and accompanied on this difficult journey. <br> We especially pray for Live Expo 2026, which will be held in August. We ask for Your guidance and protection over all the planning and preparations. May You use this event to bless all who attend. As the seasons of spring, summer, autumn, and winter illustrate the journey of life, may participants gain a deeper understanding of the value and meaning of life, and discover the wisdom found in reflecting on life, ageing, and mortality. May this event encourage meaningful conversations about life and help people approach each season with hope, purpose, and gratitude. <br><br> Gracious Lord, You are the God of grace and hope. You know the unique struggles, burdens, and difficulties that each elderly person carries in their heart. We ask that Your mighty hand would touch them, bringing comfort to their hearts and peace to their souls. Help them to fix their eyes on You alone and to rely on You, that they may find true comfort, strength, and blessing in Your presence. Whether they are living in nursing homes or receiving special care at home, Lord, may they experience Your love more deeply each day.
Lord, we also thank You for using every member of our care and visitation team as a channel of Your love. May their companionship, encouragement, and service bring joy, comfort, and the hope that comes from You to those they visit.|
