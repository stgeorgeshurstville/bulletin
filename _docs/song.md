### 4. Great is Your Faithfulness <br>
Great is Your faithfulness, O God my Father,<br>
You have fulfilled all Your promise to me;<br>
You never fail and Your love is unchanging –<br>
all You have been You for ever will be.<br><br>
 
Great is Your faithfulness, great is Your faithfulness<br>
morning by morning new mercies I see;<br>
all I have needed Your hand has provided –<br>
great is Your faithfulness, Father, to me.<br><br>
 
Summer and winter, and springtime and harvest,<br>
sun, moon and stars in their courses above,<br>
join with all nature in eloquent witness<br>
to your great faithfulness, mercy and love. <br><br>
