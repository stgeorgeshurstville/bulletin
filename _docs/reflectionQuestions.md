### Big idea


### Discussion Questions
1) Think about the ministries or activities you participate in most often at church. What is one specific, practical habit you or your group can change this week to ensure you are blessing the next person who walks into that room, rather than burdening them?

2) Have you ever separated "doing spiritual ministry" (like attending a great Bible study or leading a group) from "physical responsibility" (like resetting the chairs)? How does recognizing the "invisible labor" of others change the way you view 1 Corinthians 13 love?

3)  What is one step you can take this month to shift your mindset to "stewardship" of our church resources?

Please self-reflect on your notes.
